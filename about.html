Who We Are

Team Horus is the official solar electric vehicle team of Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bengaluru. Founded in 2017, we are a multidisciplinary student team passionate about sustainable mobility.

Our team comprises students from Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics, and Computer Science backgrounds, working together to design, build, and race solar-powered vehicles.

We believe in learning by doing – building real vehicles, not just simulations. Every component, from chassis to telemetry, is engineered by students.

Our Vision

To lead the transformation towards sustainable mobility through solar-powered innovation, inspiring the next generation of engineers to build a cleaner future.

Our Mission

To train engineers by building real vehicles, not simulations. We provide hands-on experience in design, fabrication, and competition that prepares students for industry challenges.
